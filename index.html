SquareLine

Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 2026

Overview

SquareLine is a mobile puzzle game developed by Mateus Sales Bentes. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

SquareLine does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No device identifiers are collected or sent to any server.

Data Stored Locally

The app saves the following data only on your device:

  • High scores
  • Sound and music preferences
  • Game performance statistics (used only for adaptive difficulty)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

SquareLine does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social login services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

SquareLine is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users, as no data collection takes place.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
